Welcome to Hamlet Cafe

We are a friendly, family owned and managed restaurant, located in Alamdanga close to and Alamdaga govt. Degree College.Established 2017 Musab Touhid continues to offer a very individual and unique dining experience specialising in Chicken Biye Bari, seasonal game and home made traditional Burger.

The restaurant caters for up to big areas with a separate Family Table and pleasant garden dining area to enjoy during the summer months.
